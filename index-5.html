Monuments in Uttar Pradesh


The main attraction in Uttar Pradesh is the Taj Mahal, one of the seven wonders of the world, in Agra. An immense mausoleum of white marble, the Taj Mahal is one of the universally admired masterpieces of the world's heritage. Along with that Uttar Pradesh has much more to offer. Like the Agra Fort, the masterpiece of the Mughal Dynasty and a world heritage site, and Fatehpur Sikri, an abandoned city and best-preserved examples of Mughal architecture in India. It also has many holy places: Ghats in Varanasi are considered as one of the holiest places in Varanasi, on the banks of the Ganges; and Sarnath city is located near Varanasi and an important pilgrimage site for Buddhists.
