Climate of Uttar Pradesh

Climate


The climate of the state is tropical monsoon. The average temperature varies in the plains from 3 to 4 °C in January to 43 to 45 °C in May and June. There are three distinct seasons - winter from October to February, summer from March to mid-June, and the rainy season from June to September. The rainfall in the plains is heaviest in the east and decreases towards the northwest
