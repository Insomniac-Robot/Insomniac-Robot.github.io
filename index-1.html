Topography

Location

Uttar Pradesh is a state in northern India. With over 200 million inhabitants, it is the most populous state in India as well as the most populous country subdivision in the world.
Uttar Pradesh is bordered by the state of Uttarakhand and the country of Nepal to the north, the state of Bihar to the east, the states of Jharkhand and Chhattisgarh to the southeast, the state of Madhya Pradesh to the south, and the states of Rajasthan and Haryana and the National Capital Territory of Delhi to the west.

Geographical Regions

  • The Shivalik foothills and Terai in the North

  • The Gangetic Plain in the centre - Highly fertile alluvial soils; flat topography broken by numerous ponds, lakes, and rivers; slope 2 m/km

  • The Vindhya Hills and plateau in the south - Hard rock Strata; varied topography of hills, plains, valleys, and plateau; limited water availability.
